A Focus on Sweet Service

Making customers happy one scoop at a time

 Learn lifelong customer service skills by participating in our award winning service model geared towards bringing smiles and satisfaction to your Shop’s loyal guests. From day one you will be immersed in a hands-on training to equip you with the tools, recipes and skills needed to deliver top notch customer service with each and every scoop. In addition to the training received, you’ll also benefit from competitive pay and enthusiastic recognition for a job well done.
Are you highly motivated, enthusiastic, positive, and team oriented? If so, you could make a valuable contribution to your Häagen-Dazs Shops team! Not only will you be a part of your local Shop community, you will also have the opportunity to connect and engage with other Häagen-Dazs Shop team members across the country through an interactive Shops communication app. You can share best practices, stay up to date with the latest brand news and test your product knowledge with weekly challenge quizzes. Support a Worthy Cause

Häagen-Dazs is Sweet on Honey Bees

 At Häagen-Dazs, we believe it is our duty to defend the humble honey bee. Not only do bees help pollinate over one-third of the world’s crops, they also help in the creation of one-third of our most beloved Häagen-Dazs Shop ice cream flavors. With the bee population disappearing at an alarming rate, Häagen-Dazs has been dedicated to their cause since 2008, supporting honey bee research & education and creating year-round bee friendly habitats. As a Häagen-Dazs Shops team member, you can spread the word to help keep bees buzzing.
